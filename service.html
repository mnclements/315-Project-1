    As a member of the philanthropy committee for the Epsilon Rho chapter of Kappa Kappa Gamma, I help work to raise a total of around $50,000 every school year. This money benefits both our national philanthropy, The National Alliance on Mental Illness, and our local philanthropy, Books and a Blanket. Each semester, we host an philanthropy event at our house with a raffle, food, and entertainment. If you would like to attend this semester's event or donate to our philanthropies, please visit our Crowd Change page. To learn more about our philanthropies, visit NAMI.org and the Books and a Blanket website.

    This fall I am excited to be joining the digital team at Let Them Live as an unpaid intern. Let Them Live is a non-profit that supports pregnant women facing financial struggles. A few of the ways that the organization helps mothers includes paying for their rent or car payments, providing counseling, and helping them find employment. They make this possible by hosting fundraisers for specific moms and accepting monthly donations from generous donors. I will be helping the organization this semester with optimizing their technology platforms.
